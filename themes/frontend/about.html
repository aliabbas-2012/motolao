MOTOLAO

We are a Motorcycle Tour company that prides ourselves in hosting successful, well organized on road and off road adventure tours in Laos. Our tours offer new adventures everyday and give riders a chance to journey on a road of self discovery and to experience life changing moments. Besides the exciting riding, other highlights of our tours include the chance to immerse yourself in the warm Laos culture and to experience the amazing natural beauty that Laos has to offer. MOTOLAO is a subsidiary company of Tiger Trail Laos.

TIGER TRAIL OUTDOOR ADVENTURES

Since its inception in 2000, Tiger Trail Outdoor Adventures has specialized in unique small group tours. We provide exclusive eco-adventure excursions to individuals who wish to experience and respect Lao nature, tradi- tion, and culture. Tiger Trail is a social business based in Luang Prabang aiming to reach out under the sustainable tourism approach of the Fair Trek Project, which we started to support tourism benefiting communities in the region and beyond. By joining one of our tours you automatically support this project.
